Paper Waste Reduction: Cashless and Paperless System at Beta Apartment Service and Alpha Apartment

As part of Financial Park Labuan's ongoing efforts to reduce environmental impact, Beta and Alpha Apartments have adopted cashless and paperless systems to minimize paper waste. At Beta Apartment Service, the paperless system is implemented at 50% capacity, while Alpha Apartment has fully integrated cashless payments, enabling digital transactions through mobile wallets, credit/debit cards, and online transfers.

These initiatives reduce paper waste, enhance operational efficiency, and offer greater convenience for guests and staff. By embracing modern, eco-friendly practices, both apartments align with Financial Park Labuan broader ESG goals to reduce waste and promote sustainability.
