Implementation of ABAC (No Gift Policy)

Financial Park Labuan (FPL) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and transparency through its Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption (ABAC) practices, including the adoption of a No Gift Policy. This policy prohibits the acceptance or offering of gifts, benefits, or hospitality that could influence business decisions or create conflicts of interest, ensuring all dealings are conducted with fairness and integrity.

By enforcing this policy, Financial Park Labuan promotes a culture of transparency, prevents undue influence in transactions, and ensures decisions are based on merit.
