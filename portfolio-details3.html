Investing in Local Initiatives: Internship Program

Financial Park Labuan has introduced a comprehensive internship program to provide students and young professionals with hands-on experience in various sectors. This initiative bridges academic knowledge with real-world experience, offering mentorship and skill development in areas like problem-solving and teamwork, while exposing participants to business, sustainability, and governance. It also supports the local economy by investing in human capital to reduce youth unemployment and promote long-term economic sustainability.

Aligned with Financial Park Labuan's ESG framework, this program contributes to the social and economic well-being of the local community. By empowering young talents with meaningful learning opportunities, FPL aims to create a sustainable future where individuals and the region can thrive.
